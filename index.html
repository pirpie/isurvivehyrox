HYROX is a global fitness race combining 8km of running with 8 functional workout stations.

SkiErg Sled Push Sled Pull Burpee Broad Jumps Rowing Farmers Carry Sandbag Lunges Wall Balls

It's not just a race. It's a test of everything. And the only way to get better is to track where you're losing time.

Features

Built to make you faster.

⚡
Track all 8 stations
Log your times after every session. See yourself get faster week by week.
📊
Visualize your progress
Charts that show improvement over time — so you know your training is actually working.
🏆
Real benchmarks
Compare against 700,000+ real HYROX race results. Know if you're Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, or Elite — for your age and gender.
📅
Race day countdown
Your race date. Always front and center. Every session logged is one step closer.
How to use

No download. No login.
No subscription.

Open the app → Set your race date → Start logging your sessions.
Want it on your home screen? Install it like an app — completely free.
